About Street Food🥟🥟🥟🥟

Street food is food and drinks sold by vendors in public places, like streets, markets, fairs, or parks, for immediate consumption. It's usually sold from a food cart, food truck, or portable food booth. Street food 🥟🥟🥟🥟 is often cheaper than restaurant food and is a convenient and important part of many countries' economies.We hope our shop adds a unique and intersting place to our city.

Street Food Trivia

When did street food appears first?

The most ancient evidence of food prepared and cooked on the street dates back to the dawn of civilisation, around ten thousand years ago. The ancient Greeks described the Egyptian custom, traditional in the port of Alexandria and subsequently adopted throughout Greece, of frying fish and selling it in the street.

(Source:Wikipedia)
